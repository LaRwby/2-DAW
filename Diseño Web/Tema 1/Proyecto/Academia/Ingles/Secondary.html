At the Lockser Academy we have qualified teachers to deal with all ESO subjects. We review the material covered in class so that it is clear and established and we dedicate ourselves to practicing a multitude of different exercises similar to those that may be asked of them in their exams. We have a very extensive educational material and a multitude of exams from previous courses, all posted and available to you in our virtual classroom.

Our review classes are dynamic, motivating and participatory, where the student takes center stage in their own learning, guaranteeing a comprehensive and meaningful understanding of each subject.

You can choose between:

Private face-to-face classes or in groups of a maximum of 6 students.

Private online classes or in groups of maximum 4 students.
