Intensive course for selectivity 2022

Who is the intensive course for selectivity 2022 for? Super intensive course aimed at all students who have completed the baccalaureate within the educational system in Spain or who have completed a Higher Level Training Cycle and want to take the entrance exams to the University Are you finishing high school and want to pass the Selectivity? You risk being able to choose the career of your dreams... are you afraid? nerves?. Don't worry.

Prepare yourself with this course and no Selectivity question will surprise you. Pass selectivity with the Super Intensive Guiu course in Online mode. You can change to the face-to-face modality, if you wish, when the time comes. The course is structured in two blocks of 4 weeks plus a final week of doubts and questions, and will have a modular and cyclical nature, adapted to the Online modality, so that you can join the course at any time.
